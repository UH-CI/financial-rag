Your Committee on
Finance, to which was referred H.B. No. 1169, H.D. 1, entitled:
"A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII
CONFERENCE CENTER REVOLVING FUND,"
begs leave to report as follows:
The
purpose of this measure is to:
(1)
Establish the
University of Hawaii Conference Center Revolving Fund to facilitate the
administration of conference center programs among various campuses and
operating units of the University of Hawaii System;
(2)
Repeal the Conference
Center Revolving Fund for the University of Hawaii at Hilo and Community
College Conference Center Revolving Fund and lapse all unencumbered funds to
the University of Hawaii Conference Center Revolving Fund; and
(3)
Appropriate funds out
of the University of Hawaii Conference Center Revolving Fund.
