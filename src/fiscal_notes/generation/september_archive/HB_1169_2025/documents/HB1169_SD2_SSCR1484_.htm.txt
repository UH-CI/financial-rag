Sir:
Your Committee on
Ways and Means, to which was referred H.B. No. 1169, H.D. 1,
S.D. 1, entitled:
"A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII
CONFERENCE CENTER REVOLVING FUND,"
begs leave to report as follows:
The
purpose and intent of this measure is to establish the university of Hawaii conference
center revolving fund and repeal two existing conference center funds.
Your Committee
received written comments in support of this measure from the University of
Hawaii System.
Your Committee
finds that establishing a single revolving fund for all conference center
activities will help to standardize conference center programs across all
campuses of the University of Hawaii System and facilitate the administration
of conference center moneys.
Your Committee has
amended this measure by
(1)
Clarifying the permissible uses of
moneys appropriated from the university of Hawaii conference center revolving
fund
; and
(2)
Making technical nonsubstantive
amendments for purposes of clarity, consistency, and style.
As affirmed by the
record of votes of the members of your Committee on Ways and Means that is
attached to this report, your Committee is in accord with the intent and
purpose of H.B. No. 1169, H.D. 1, S.D. 1, as amended herein, and
recommends that it pass Third Reading in the form attached hereto as H.B.
No. 1169, H.D. 1, S.D. 2.
Respectfully submitted on behalf
of the members of the Committee on Ways and Means,
