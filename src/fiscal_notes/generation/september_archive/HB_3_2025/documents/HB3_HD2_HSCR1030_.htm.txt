Your Committee on Finance,
to which was referred H.B. No. 3, H.D. 1, entitled:
"A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO BEACH MANAGEMENT ON
THE NORTH SHORE OF OAHU,"
begs leave to report as follows:
The
purpose of this measure is to extend the deadline for the University of Hawaii
Sea Grant College Program to submit the North Shore Beach Management Plan to
the Governor and Legislature.
Your Committee received testimony in support of this measure
from the Department of Land and Natural Resources and one individual.
Your Committee received comments on this
measure from the University of Hawai

30, 2026, for the University of Hawaii Sea Grant College Program to develop the
North Shore Beach Management Plan; and
(2)
Making technical, nonsubstantive amendments
for the purposes of clarity, consistency, and style.
As affirmed by the
record of votes of the members of your Committee on Finance that is attached to
this report, your Committee is in accord with the intent and purpose of H.B.
No. 3, H.D. 1, as amended herein, and recommends that it pass Third Reading in
the form attached hereto as H.B. No. 3, H.D. 2.
