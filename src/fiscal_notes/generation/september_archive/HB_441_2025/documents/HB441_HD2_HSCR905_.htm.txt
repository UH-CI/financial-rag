Madame:
Your Committee on Finance,
to which was referred H.B. No. 441, H.D. 2, entitled:
"A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO CIGARETTE
TAXES,"
begs leave to report as follows:
The
purpose of this measure is to increase the cigarette tax beginning July 1,
2025, and allocate the increase in the cigarette tax revenues into certain special
funds.
Your Committee received testimony in support of this measure
from the Department of Health; University of Hawai
ʻ
i
Cancer Center; Hawaii Substance Abuse Coalition; Hawai
ʻ
i
Pacific Health; The Queen's Health Systems; Hawaii Medical Association; and
nine individuals.
Your Committee
received testimony in opposition to this measure from Kainehe Market; Kilani
Market; Bionic Petroleum; China Town Liquor; Cigar Association of America,
Inc.; Marukin Market; Retail Merchants of Hawaii; Discount Smoke Shop Hawaii; KCL
Aloha Pacific Inc.; No. 1 Store; S&K Wholesale; Wawa Village Market;
Young's Wine and Spirit; Hawaii Smokers Alliance; and four individuals.
Your Committee received comments on this
measure from the Department of the Attorney General; Department of Taxation;
State Health Planning and Development Agency; Hina Mauka; Hawaii COPD
Coalition; Campaign for Tobacco-Free Kids; Tobacco-Free Kids Action Fund; Tax
Foundation; Tax Foundation of Hawaii; Coalition for a Tobacco-Free Hawai
ʻ
i;
American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network; Taxpayers Protection Alliance;
Hawai
ʻ
i Health & Harm Reduction Center; and
eight individuals.
As affirmed by the
record of votes of the members of your Committee on Finance that is attached to
this report, your Committee is in accord with the intent and purpose of H.B.
No. 441, H.D. 2, and recommends that it pass Third Reading.
Respectfully
submitted on behalf of the members of the Committee on Finance,
