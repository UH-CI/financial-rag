Madam and Sir:
Your Committee on
Conference on the disagreeing vote of the House of Representatives to the
amendments proposed by the Senate in H.B. No. 990, H.D. 2,
S.D. 1, entitled:
"A BILL FOR AN ACT MAKING APPROPRIATIONS FOR CLAIMS
AGAINST THE STATE, ITS OFFICERS, OR ITS EMPLOYEES,"
having met, and after full and free discussion, has agreed
to recommend and does recommend to the respective Houses the final passage of
this bill in an amended form.
The purpose of
this measure is to satisfy several claims against the State, its officers, or
its employees.
Your Committee on
Conference has amended this measure by:
(1)
Appropriating:
(A)
$69,113.33 in general funds for a claim by
Gilbert Collins;
(B)
$166,492.00 in general funds for a claim by National
Commerce Bank Service;
(C)
$50,210.67 in general funds for a claim by Olinda
Country Estates, Inc.;
(D)
$22,674.17 in general funds for a claim by Sofia
Lerman Estate;
(E)
$100,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of Donna Opulento v. State of Hawaii Department of Public Safety;
(F)
$100,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of D.O. v. State of Hawaii, Department of Education;
(G)
$140,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of Leonard Ramboyon v. Esther Kanehailua;
(H)
$50,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of Jon R. Chung v. State of Hawaii;
(I)
$298,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the tort claim by Kamaka Air, LLC;
(J)
$512,700.83 in general funds for a judgment in
the case of John Roe No. 121 v. State of Hawaii;
(K)
$115,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of Anela Aplaca v. State of Hawaii;
(L)
$125,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of Stacy J. Bruhn v. State of Hawaii;
(M)
$26,771.00 in general funds for a judgment in
the case of Healoha Carmichael v. Board of Land and Natural Resources;
(N)
$18,000.00 in general funds for a settlement
in the case of Kona Mike's Surf Adventures, LLC v. Dawn N.S. Chang; and
(O)
$70,000.00 out of the state highway fund for a
settlement in the case of Susanna Sigal v. State of Hawaii;
(2)
Amending the amount and percentage of
exceedance of the appropriations over the general fund expenditure ceiling for
fiscal year 2024-2025;
(3)
Changing the effective date to upon approval;
and
(4)
Making technical, nonsubstantive amendments
for the purposes of clarity, consistency, and style.
As affirmed by the
record of votes of the managers of your Committee on Conference that is
attached to this report, your Committee on Conference is in accord with the
intent and purpose of H.B. No. 990, H.D. 2, S.D. 1, as amended
herein, and recommends that it pass Final Reading in the form attached hereto
as H.B. No. 990, H.D. 2, S.D. 1, C.D. 1.
Respectfully submitted on behalf
of the managers:
