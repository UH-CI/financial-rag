Madam and Sir:
Your Committee on
Conference on the disagreeing vote of the House of Representatives to the
amendments proposed by the Senate in H.B. No. 934, H.D. 1,
S.D. 2, entitled:
"A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE HAWAII BROADBAND
AND DIGITAL EQUITY OFFICE,"
having met, and after full and free discussion, has agreed
to recommend and does recommend to the respective Houses the final passage of
this bill in an amended form.
The purpose of
this measure is to:
(1)
Rename the Hawaii Broadband and Digital Equity
Office as the Broadband Office and transfer the Office from the Department of
Business, Economic Development, and Tourism to the Office of Enterprise
Technology Services in the Department of Accounting and General Services;
(2)
Repeal broadband duties assigned to the
Director of Commerce and Consumer Affairs;
(3)
Repeal the Broadband Assistance Advisory
Council;
(4)
Transfer the duty of collecting and posting
certain broadband infrastructure notices to the Broadband Office;
(5)
Establish the Broadband Revolving Fund; and
(6)
Appropriate funds into and out of the
Broadband Revolving Fund for the reimbursement of contracts executed by the
Broadband Office and to establish Digital Navigator positions.
Your Committee on
Conference has amended this measure by:
(1)
Establishing the Broadband Office within the
Department of Accounting and General Services instead of specifically in the
Office of Enterprise and Technology Services;
(2)
Clarifying that the Broadband Office shall
advise the Comptroller, instead of the Chief Information Officer, on broadband
deployment;
(3)
Clarifying that expenditures from the
Broadband Revolving Fund shall be made by the Broadband Office advised by the
Information Technology Steering Committee and subject to prior approval by the
Comptroller;
(4)
Inserting an appropriation amount of $800,000 out
of the Broadband Revolving Fund to fund and establish six full-time equivalent Digital
Navigator positions;
(5)
Changing the effective date to July 1, 2025;
and
(6)
Making technical,
nonsubstantive amendments for the purposes of clarity, consistency, and style.
As affirmed by the
record of votes of the managers of your Committee on Conference that is
attached to this report, your Committee on Conference is in accord with the
intent and purpose of H.B. No. 934, H.D. 1, S.D. 2, as amended
herein, and recommends that it pass Final Reading in the form attached hereto
as H.B. No. 934, H.D. 1, S.D. 2, C.D. 1.

of the managers:
